About

Alli Ho is a PhD Candidate at the Scripps Institution of Oceanography studying how surface waves move and interact with the ocean and the atmosphere. She is interested in swell generation and propagation across ocean basins, wave modulation by underlying tides and bathymetry, the importance of wave-current and air-sea interactions in extreme weather, and impacts on shoreline communities.
